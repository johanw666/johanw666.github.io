"Hack Value" -- best high-tech entry 
Most of the entries in this category were based on hiding the DVD source code in an image or sound file. If you
use the World Wide Web, you might have a copy of the source code in your browser cache right now. The judges
decided to give the award to Andrea Gnesutta's entry at http://members.xoom.com/e98/ because of the quality of
the concealment and the ease of distribution. (To extract css-auth.tar.gz from dvd_scdc_tst.png, go to byte
00002428 and save in a file the next 547E bytes.)
